About Our Company

Bridging the Gap Between Complex Plant Operations and Enterprise Intelligence

Company Overview

Integra OT Technologies is a specialized engineering and technology integration company focused on Industrial Automation, AVEVA PI System, SCADA, Power BI, Industrial Cybersecurity and IT-OT Integration.

We help organizations transform operational data into actionable business intelligence through secure, scalable and high-performance industrial data platforms. Our expertise spans from plant-floor instrumentation and SCADA systems to enterprise reporting and advanced analytics.

Our mission is to connect people, processes and technology through reliable industrial data infrastructure that enables real-time visibility, operational excellence and informed decision-making.

Our Mission

To empower industries with secure and resilient IT-OT data ecosystems that provide real-time operational visibility, enable data-driven decision-making and accelerate digital transformation.

Our Vision

To become a trusted global partner in industrial digital transformation, helping organizations unlock the full potential of operational data, advanced analytics and intelligent automation.
